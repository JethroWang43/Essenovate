Digital Printing

Ink Types:

  • Toner-based ink
    Powdered ink fused onto paper using heat.
  • Inkjet ink
    Liquid ink sprayed onto paper.
  • UV-curable ink
    Cured with UV light for fast drying and durability.
  • Solvent and Eco-Solvent Ink
    Used for outdoor signage and banners due to water resistance.

Paper Types:

  • Coated Paper
    Glossy, matte, or silk finishes for sharp, vibrant images.
  • Uncoated Paper
    More natural, absorbent, and ideal for writing.
  • Cardstock
    Thicker, used for business cards, invitations, and postcards.
  • Specialty Papers
    Textured, recycled, or synthetic papers for unique projects.

Book Binding

Binding Types:

  • Toner-based ink
    Powdered ink fused onto paper using heat.
  • Heated Spine (Perfect Binding or Thermal Binding)
    Pages are glued to a spine using a strong, heated adhesive (such as PUR or EVA glue).
    The cover is then wrapped around and pressed firmly until the glue dries.
  • Wire Binding (Wire-O or Spiral Binding)
    Holes are punched along the edge of the pages, and a metal wire or plastic spiral is inserted to hold them together.

Laminating

Lamination Types:

  • Glossy Lamination
    Enhances color vibrancy and shine.
  • Matte Lamination
    Offers a smooth, non-glare finish for a premium feel.
  • Soft-Touch Lamination
    Provides a velvety texture, often used for luxury products.

Paper Types:

  • UV Coating
    A liquid coating hardened with UV light for a protective, glossy effect.
  • Encapsulation
    A thicker lamination that seals the edges for complete waterproofing.

Cutting & Finishing

Finishing Types:

  • Straight Cutting
    Standard trimming for books, flyers, and posters.
  • Die Cutting
    Custom-shaped cutting for unique designs (e.g., rounded business cards, stickers).

Paper Types:

  • Perforation
    Creates tear-off sections, commonly used for tickets and coupons.
  • Scoring & Creasing
    Prepares paper for easy folding without cracking.
